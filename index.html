What Are Anti-Wrinkle Injections?

Anti-wrinkle injections, commonly known as Botox®, are a popular non-surgical cosmetic procedure that reduces the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles. By temporarily relaxing the facial muscles responsible for creating dynamic wrinkles, these injections can smooth out expression lines and prevent new ones from forming.

Benefits of Anti-Wrinkle Injections

  • Quick and Convenient: Treatments are typically completed in less than 30 minutes, making it easy to fit into your busy schedule.
  • Minimally Invasive: With no downtime required, you can return to your daily activities immediately after the procedure.
  • Natural Results: Our skilled practitioners ensure that you maintain natural facial expressions while enjoying a more youthful look.
  • Preventative: Regular treatments can help prevent the deepening of existing wrinkles and delay the formation of new ones.

Areas Treated with Anti-Wrinkle Injections

Anti-wrinkle injections can effectively treat a variety of facial areas, including:

  • Forehead Lines: Smooth out horizontal lines across the forehead.
  • Frown Lines: Reduce the appearance of vertical lines between the eyebrows (glabellar lines).
  • Crow's Feet: Minimize the fine lines around the outer corners of the eyes.
  • Bunny Lines: Address the wrinkles that appear on the bridge of the nose.
  • Chin Dimpling: Smooth out the texture of the chin for a more even appearance.

Our Process

  1. Consultation: During your initial consultation, our experienced practitioners will discuss your aesthetic goals, assess your facial anatomy, and develop a personalized treatment plan.
  2. Treatment: The injection process involves a few tiny injections into the targeted muscles. The procedure is relatively painless, with most patients experiencing only minor discomfort.
  3. Aftercare: Post-treatment, you may notice slight redness or swelling, which typically subsides within a few hours. We provide you with detailed aftercare instructions to ensure optimal results.

What is Botox?

Botox is a highly effective, non-surgical treatment that reduces the appearance of wrinkles and fine lines by temporarily relaxing the underlying facial muscles. Commonly treated areas include:

  • Forehead lines
  • Crow's feet around the eyes
  • Frown lines between the eyebrows

With Botox, you can enjoy smoother, younger-looking skin with results that can last up to four months.

Discover the Benefits of Dermal Fillers

Dermal fillers are another popular option for combating the signs of aging. These injectable treatments restore volume to the face, smooth out deep wrinkles, and enhance facial contours. At Ziya Skin Clinic, we use premium fillers that provide natural-looking results. Dermal fillers can be used to:

  • Enhance shallow contours

  • Improve the appearance of recessed scars
